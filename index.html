Technical Analysis

In this swift course we will be covering the fundementals of understanding Technical Analysis.

Technical Analysis is used to make price predictions based on historical data. Often price action patterns repeat in financial markets, and are therefor predictable.

Understanding Candlesticks

Understanding candlesticks is the basis of technical analysis. There are more options like line charts, bar charts, renko bars but candlestick charts provide more then enough information.

The first step is to differentiate bullish and bearish candlesticks:
Bullish candlesticks refer to green up trending candles.
Bearish candlesticks refer to red down trending candles.
